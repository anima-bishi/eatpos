About Us

We Want You And Your Customers To Feel Confident About Reopening

The eatPOS All In One App has been designed and enhanced for COVID-19 by software developers from the hospitality industry, which means we’re on the same page as you. We understand how important it is to simplify your customer’s ordering process as well as making it easy for both front of house and kitchen staff to manage. The eatPOS software allows for complete contactless ordering be it order at table, order at home, click and collect, room service, or takeaway! With our expertise and experience we ensure contactless ordering is implemented smoothly within your business and enriches a customer’s ordering experience. Our All In One App is customised to your business needs and house style whether your business is wet- or dry-led/both.
